## Background Information

On August 27, 2021 we had 782 participants. 52.9% of the participants are female, and 47.1% male. The majority of the participants (83.9%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 5.1% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 11% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.

## Weekly Symptoms

On August 27, 2021 we had 8109 completed weekly symptom questionnaires from 726 participants. In 81.7% of the symptom questionnaires it was reported that there were *no symptoms*.
