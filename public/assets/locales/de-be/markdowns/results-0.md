## Background Information

On July 6, 2021 we had 697 participants. 53.1% of the participants are female, and 46.9% male. The majority of the participants (84.1%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 4.6% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 11.4% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.

## Weekly Symptoms

On July 6, 2021 we had 5237 completed weekly symptom questionnaires from 646 participants. In 79.1% of the symptom questionnaires it was reported that there were *no symptoms*.
