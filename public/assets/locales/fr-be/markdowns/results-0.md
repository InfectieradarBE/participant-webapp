## Background Information

On June 1, 2021 we had 589 participants. 53.7% of the participants are female, and 46.3% male. The majority of the participants (83.9%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 4.8% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 11.4% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.

## Weekly Symptoms

On June 1, 2021 we had 3285 completed weekly symptom questionnaires from 545 participants. In 76,9% of the symptom questionnaires it was reported that there were *no symptoms*.
