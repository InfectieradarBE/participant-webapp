## Background Information

On June 30, 2021 we had 682 participants. 53.4% of the participants are female, and 46.6% male. The majority of the participants (83.9%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 4.7% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 11.4% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.

## Weekly Symptoms

On June 30, 2021 we had 4907 completed weekly symptom questionnaires from 634 participants. In 78.9% of the symptom questionnaires it was reported that there were *no symptoms*.
