## Background Information

On August 17, 2021 we had 764 participants. 53.3% of the participants are female, and 46.7% male. The majority of the participants (83.8%) reported having no previous Covid-19 episode. 5.2% of the participants had a positive Covid-19 test in the past. 11.2% of the participants think they had a previous Covid-19 episode.

## Weekly Symptoms

On August 17, 2021 we had 7572 completed weekly symptom questionnaires from 711 participants. In 81.3% of the symptom questionnaires it was reported that there were *no symptoms*.
